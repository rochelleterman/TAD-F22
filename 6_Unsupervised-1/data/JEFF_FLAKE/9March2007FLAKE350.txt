REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

BYLINE: US Fed News

LENGTH: 71  words

DATELINE: WASHINGTON


Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following press release:

Republican Rep. Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District, today
highlighted a pork project contained in the agriculture appropriations bill for
fiscal year 2006.

This week's egregious earmark: $1,000,000 for beef improvement research in
Missouri and Texas.

"This earmark is a bunch of bull," said Flake.
