REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

BYLINE: US Fed News

LENGTH: 68  words

DATELINE: WASHINGTON


Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following press release:

Rep. Jeff Flake, who represents the Arizona's Sixth District, today highlighted
a pork project contained in the Interior appropriations bill for fiscal year
2006.

This week's egregious earmark: $140,000 for the Woodward Opera House in Ohio.

"Pork projects like this defy Carmen-sense," said Flake.
