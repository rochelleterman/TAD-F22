REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

LENGTH: 95  words


WASHINGTON, Nov. 6 -- Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following
press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted a pork project contained in the omnibus spending bill for
fiscal year 2009.

This week's egregious earmark: $238,000 for equipment and technology upgrades at
Finger Lakes Community College in New York.

"If Congress had their finger on the pulse of taxpayers, they'd stop
earmarking," said Flake.For more information please contact: Sarabjit Jagirdar,
