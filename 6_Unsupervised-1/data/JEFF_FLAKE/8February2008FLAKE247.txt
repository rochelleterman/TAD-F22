REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF WEEK

BYLINE: US Fed News

LENGTH: 106  words

DATELINE: MESA, Ariz.


Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted a pork project contained in the Omnibus Appropriations bill
for fiscal year 2008.

This week's egregious earmark: $316,000 for Palmer College on Chiropractice,
Consortial Center for Chiropractic Research in Davenport, Iowa and the Policy
Institute for Integrative Medicine in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania for a best
practices initiative on lower back pain.

"Isn't it about time that the earmark process had an adjustment?" said Flake.
