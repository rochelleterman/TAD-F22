REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARKS OF THE WEEK

BYLINE: US Fed News

LENGTH: 77  words

DATELINE: WASHINGTON


Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following press release:

Rep. Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District, today highlighted pork
projects contained in the Transportation-Treasury-HUD appropriations bill for
fiscal year 2006.

This week's egregious earmarks: $50,000 to Homer, Pennsylvania for construction
of a new athletic facility.

"Pork projects like this make voters want to scream, 'D'oh!,'" said Flake.
