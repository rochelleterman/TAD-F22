REP. CONGRESSMAN FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF WEEK

LENGTH: 101  words


WASHINGTON, Jan. 8 -- Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following
press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted an earmark contained in the omnibus spending bill for fiscal
year 2010, which the House of Representatives passed last month.

This week's egregious earmark: $600,000 for coastal tidal gauges in Alabama.

"The Crimson Tide may be national champs, but the tide has yet to turn in
Congress when it comes to earmark reform," said Flake.For more information
