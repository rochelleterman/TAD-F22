REP. FLAKE TO HOST RECEPTION IN HONOR OF CONGRESSIONAL HIGH SCHOOL ART
COMPETITION PARTICIPANTS, WINNERS

LENGTH: 255  words


WASHINGTON, April 6 -- Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following
press release:

Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District, will host a
reception in honor of students who participated in the 2010 Congressional Art
Award Contest entitled, "An Artistic Discovery." This event is a non-partisan
contest for the most talented young artists in Arizona's Sixth District. The
three competition winners for 2010 will be announced at the reception.

The first place winner will receive the honor of having a piece of their
original artwork displayed for one year in a corridor in the United States
Capitol building. In addition to the Capitol display, the first place entry will
be eligible upon acceptance to receive a $1,500 admission scholarship to the
prestigious Savannah College of Art and Design in Savannah, Georgia. The second
and third place winners will have their artwork displayed in Congressman Flake's
Washington, D.C. office and in his District Office, respectively.

All high school students, their families, and art enthusiasts are welcome to
celebrate with the contest participants and winners. Questions can be directed
to Mary Baumbach in Congressman Flake's District office at 480-833-0092.
