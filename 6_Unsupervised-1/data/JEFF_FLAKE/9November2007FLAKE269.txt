REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

BYLINE: US Fed News

LENGTH: 84  words

DATELINE: WASHINGTON


Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted a pork project contained in the Labor-HHS Appropriations bill
for fiscal year 2008.

This week's egregious earmark: $169,500 for the Albert Einstein Health Network
in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania for college student screening programs.

"This sounds like just another wild-haired earmark," said Flake.
