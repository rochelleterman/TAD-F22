REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

LENGTH: 86  words


MESA, Ariz., June 5 -- Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following
press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted a pork project contained in the omnibus spending bill for
fiscal year 2009. This week's egregious earmark: $100,000 to the West Point
Historic Post Office in West Point, Mississippi. "Taxpayers to Congress: Return
