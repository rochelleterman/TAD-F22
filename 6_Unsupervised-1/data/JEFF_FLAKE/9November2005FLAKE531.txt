REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

BYLINE: US Fed News

LENGTH: 82  words

DATELINE: WASHINGTON


Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following press release:

Arizona Rep. Jeff Flake, who represents the state's Sixth District, today
highlighted a pork project contained in the Agriculture spending bill for fiscal
year 2006.

This week's egregious earmark: $591,000 for tri-state joint peanut research in
Alabama.

"With Congress continuing to shell out money for earmarks like this, it's no
wonder we're driving taxpayers nuts," said Flake.
