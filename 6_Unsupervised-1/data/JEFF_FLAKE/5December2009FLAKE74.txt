REP. FLAKE SPOTLIGHTS EGREGIOUS EARMARK OF THE WEEK

LENGTH: 91  words


WASHINGTON, Dec. 4 -- Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz. (6th CD), issued the following
press release:

Republican Congressman Jeff Flake, who represents Arizona's Sixth District,
today highlighted a pork project contained in the omnibus spending bill for
fiscal year 2009.

This week's egregious earmark: $866,000 for stable fly control in Lincoln,
Nebraska.

"If taxpayers are looking for a stable economy, earmarks like this just aren't
going to fly," said Flake.For more information please contact: Sarabjit
